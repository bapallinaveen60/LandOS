Technology

How Spatial AI Solves Hydrology

Traditional tabular machine learning fails to model directional water flow. LND-11 solves this using spatial structures:

  • Graph Neural Networks (GraphSAGE): Models land parcels as nodes on river catchments and watershed networks to predict downstream accumulation and flooding.
  • Conformal Prediction: Ensures that every output score includes calibrated confidence intervals to establish statistical reliability for institutional finance.
  • S3 GeoParquet Lakehouse: Enables sub-second parallel spatial lookups over terabytes of historical weather grids.

The Data Synthesis Loop

1. User Input -> Cadastral Boundary Georeferenced.

2. HTTP Range Requests scan COGs in Amazon S3.

3. Parallel Zonal Stats extracted on EKS Workers.

4. Pre-trained models predict calibrated probabilities in under 2 seconds.
